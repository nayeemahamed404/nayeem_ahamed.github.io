About Me

Hello! I'm Nayeem, a web developer based in Dhaka, Bangladesh. My journey into web development started with a fascination for how things work on the internet. This curiosity quickly grew into a passion for building elegant and functional websites.

I specialize in front-end development, with a strong command of HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and modern frameworks like React. I also have experience with back-end technologies such as Node.js and Express, allowing me to build full-stack applications.

I'm dedicated to continuous learning and always excited to explore new technologies to enhance my skills. When I'm not coding, I enjoy exploring new cafes, reading tech blogs, and contributing to open-source projects.
